Hitachi Data Systems - Partner Portal

Roles: Concept, Wireframe, UX/UI Design, Functional Design, Technical Design, Technical Implementation, DevOps Activities

Working on the Partner Portal has been my longest-standing project with Hitachi Data Systems. For the first year of my career here, I was primarily tasked with content contribution, but certain aspects of the framework, and especially the User Interface/User Experience, led me to believe there were certainly opportunities for improvement.

For the next two years, I worked on several functional prototypes while continually improving the existing site through better UI design and simpler Information Architecture. We could measurably observe improvements from user satisfaction ratings, system response times, and the time it took for users to find information.

At the same time, I performed a role somewhat akin to a Business Analysts's and assisted with the functional design of a replacement Partner Portal.

Nearly four years since I began my career at Hitachi Data Systems, I was granted an opportunity to be a key player of the IT technical team that deployed and released the new Partner Portal on the Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) platform. We had come a long way since our open-source software days. Assisting with the design, implementation, and eventual deployment of the site remains the proudest and most humbling episode of my life to date.

My day-to-day duties still include designing and developing improvements to the Portal.






Hitachi Data Systems - Responsive/Mobile Optimized Site

Roles: Concept, Wireframe, UX/UI Design, Technical Development

Perhaps the largest project I've undertaken and spearheaded. To sum it up, this is a one-man design and development team reworking the corporate website.

As corporations continue to embrace modern web technologies and standards, I felt it was time to inject some new life into the corporate website as well as the Partner Portal. What began as simple hand-drawn wireframes turned into a full-blown functional mockup in just a matter of days.

The mockup served the basis for the new Hitachi Data Systems website which was deployed and launched in April of 2015 on the Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) platform.

Hitachi Data Systems - Solutions Strategy

Roles: UX/UI/IA Design, Technical Development

As Hitachi Data Systems continues to evolve and remain ahead of the curve, it wanted to realign its corporate messaging around its solutions. In a year-long project, half of which was dedicated to designing a clear and concise Information Architecture, we managed to create an intuitive and dynamic user experience, doing away with the old unordered list of offerings with an industry-standard use-case categorization.
