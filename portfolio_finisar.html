Welcome to Design Engineering.

Finisar Corporation (NASDAQ: FNSR)

Roles: Mechanical Design Engineer

As someone who had been particularly interested in furthering my experience in DFA/DFM, working directly in the manufacturing team at Finisar lent me significant insight into the processes that a major corporation utilizes to produce electronic components. I constantly communicated with technicians on the assembly line, trying to understand pain-points which assisted me in formulating design problems (customer needs) which I then addressed with actual prototypes.

Designing parts virtually with the help of CAD software (SolidWorks, in my case) dramatically reduces time spent on custom-fitting components, allowing me to spend more time on making sure that whatever I design will stand up to the user requirements. Not only that, but my design philosophy places a high emphasis on simplicity and user friendliness. I've spent enough time behind the controls of a manual Bridgeport mill to know gruesome it can be to make certain parts; that knowledge helps me design parts that not only function well, but are easy to make. Cost reduction is always a welcome byproduct!
